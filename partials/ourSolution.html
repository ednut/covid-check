Our Solution

Eduplan aims to develop soft skills training in students to reduce out-of-school children in rural communities. By developing their mental abilities, they can develop the skills to overcome different life challenges in their family, school and community.

  • Quality Education Campaign
  • Out-of-school Children Project
  • Student Online Learning and Development
  • Eduplan Volunteer Initiative

We aim with these enlyteen solutions to provide academic opportunities to students who hope for a better life, reduce poverty and contribute positively to national development.
